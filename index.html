I, America

This is an attempt to capture some of the many faces of diversity in America by giving a voice to the stories of second-generation Americans. Through stories we can share human feelings of joy, sadness, shame, and triumph; through stories, we see each other for who we are, rather than for who the world says we are. It is my hope to portray the beauty of diversity by focusing on the lessons we can learn from other cultures about ourselves and the world.

Anna Wang
